About Us

The Story Behind Vixies

We are an independent creative & tech organization born from student passion — turning wild ideas into real creations.

From Campus Chats to a Creative Ecosystem

Vixies Team started as casual conversations among college friends and grew into a community that believes in cross-disciplinary collaboration. Here, coders, designers, writers, and gamers unite to create something impactful.

Our vision is to bridge the academic world and the industry through open projects that anyone can learn from and develop together. We're not just a study group — we're a small ecosystem where experiments become real products.
